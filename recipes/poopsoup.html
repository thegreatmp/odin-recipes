Busted Magic Draft, a.k.a. Poop Soup

What starts with the best of intentions quickly snowballs out of control as your colors dry up. Scrapping for D-level playables and awash with regret and confusion, the resultant concoction can best be described as "Poop Soup".

Ingredients

  • 1500 gems
  • Inflated ego
  • Rudimentary knowledge of format draft signals

Preparation

  1. Begin the draft.
  2. Notice a seemingly open lane about 3 to 5 picks in.
  3. Commit to that lane. Be sure to get your hopes up about the potential.
  4. Around pick 10, you should start to feel the dread rising as the final picks yield nothing playable.
  5. Reaffirm your mental commitment to making the plan work.
  6. Open crap in Pack 2.

    7. Try this for a neat twist:

    • Hard pivot after opening an off color bomb in pack 2!

  7. Force your way into a second color because you expect to see it in Pack 3.
  8. Observe the great cards getting passed to you in two colors you are not playing.
  9. Wallow in self pity as the plan utterly fails to materialize.
  10. Consider dropping without playing a game.
  11. Play the games, because value.
  12. Lose the games.
