Just Coffee

The nectar of life and the main purpose for waking up in the morning. Prepare twice as much as you expect to need - life is full of surprises.

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans
  • Clean water
  • Coffee maker of your preference

Preparation

  1. Add coffee to your coffee maker..
  2. Add water to your coffee maker.
  3. Operate your coffee maker.
  4. Drink until your resting heart rate goes above 150bpm.
